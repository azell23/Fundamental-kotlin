##Operasi matematika
|Operator|Keterangan|
 |-------|----------|
|+   |penjumlahan|
|-| pengurangan|
|*   |perkalian |
|/|pembagian |
|% |sisa bagi |
```aidl
val tambah: Int=10+2
val bagi: Int=10/2
```
|Operasi matematika|Augmented Assignment|
 |-------|----------|
|a=a+10|a+=10|
|a=a-10| a-=10|
|a=a*10|a*=10 |
|a=a/10|a/=10 |
|a=a%10 |a%=10 |
```aidl
var a: Int=10
a+=10
println(a)
```
##Unary Operator
|Operator|Keterangan|
 |-------|----------|
|++   |a=a+1|
|--| a=a-1|
|-   |negatif |
|+|positive |
|! |boolean kebalikan |
```aidl
var a: Int=10
a++ 
```
